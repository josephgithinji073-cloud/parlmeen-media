About Pearlmeen Media

Using African storytelling to inspire positive change

Who We Are

Pearlmeen Media is an online African storytelling company committed to amplifying authentic African voices. We use media as a tool to inform, inspire, and mobilize communities toward social, economic, and environmental change.

Our Mission

To tell stories that inspire people and communities to come together for positive and sustainable change.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading storytelling company creating impact, collaboration, and transformation through powerful narratives.

Our Values

  • Trusted by our communities
  • Committed to seeking accurate information
  • Respectful of cultures and communities
  • Delivering quality in every story
  • Captivating and engaging storytelling
